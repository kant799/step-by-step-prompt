STEP 01

Set the Stage

Begin by setting the core prompt as your 'System Prompt'. This crucial first step establishes the creative direction and technical foundation for the AI, ensuring every output aligns with the design philosophy.

STEP 02

Provide Your Content

In the chat dialog, simply send your step-by-step instructions, segmented content, or your core idea. The AI is designed to interpret and structure your input into a clear, linear flow. The rest is magic.

STEP 03

Customize with Finesse

This is where the true power lies. Refine the output by specifying your desired color scheme in the conversation (e.g., "use a warm, sunset palette") or requesting a different language for the final presentation. It's that simple!
